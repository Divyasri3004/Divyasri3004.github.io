Exhibition Details

CHROMATIC CHAOS

Where paint splatters meet existential crises | Annual Student Art Exhibition

The Madness Behind the Method

This year's exhibition explores the beautiful collision of order and chaos through 47 student artists who may or may not have slept in the studio. Witness paintings that question reality, sculptures that defy gravity, and installations that may or may not be trash (it's art if we say it is).

Don't-Miss Features

  • "The Void Stares Back" - An interactive mirror installation that judges you
  • "Caffeinated Dreams" - A sculpture made entirely of coffee cups (we have a problem)
  • "404 Creativity Not Found" - Blank canvases representing artist's block (deep, right?)

Curator's Note

"We asked our students to push boundaries, and they responded by gluing random objects to canvases and calling it 'post-modern deconstructionism.' We're so proud."
- Prof. Vyjayanthi, Department Head
